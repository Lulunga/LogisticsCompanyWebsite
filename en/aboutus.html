About us

Silen Logistics is a fast developing, young, dynamic freight forwarding company. We provide ideal schemes of cargo delivery from any point in the world and individual approach to each carriage. We guarantee the quality of our services.
Today Silen Logistics provides transport services for export and import activities of many companies.


>Key components of successful activity:

  • commitment to the long-lasting mutually profitable relations with our customers;
  • control of quality and efficiency of cargo delivery;
  • prompt reaction to the customers’ claims and wishes;
  • 100% responsibility for the cargo;
  • highly professional staff;
  • competitive prices.
